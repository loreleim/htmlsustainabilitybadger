Meet Badger the Badging Badger

An informative fellow from the respectable land of Chicago.

A sustainability scavenger hunt:

Stickers of Badger have been placed all around Chicago. These stickers are in locations where people could practice sustainable behavior. The stickers provide tips for living a more sustainable lifestyle relevant to the location in which they are stuck. Every sticker you find will earn you a unique badge once you have scanned it. Good luck trying to find them all!
