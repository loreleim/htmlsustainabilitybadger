What you can do to help

  • Loads of people unknowingly raise their power bills by leaving appliances plugged-in when they are not being used.

  • One way I've seen people reduce energy use is by unplugging appliances or using a power strip with an on/off switch.

  • An interesting thing about Badgers is that they are nocturnal, so always turn off the lights if you'll be out of the room more than 15 minutes. For the sake of the badgers.
